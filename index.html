Sally's Salmon Cookies

Did you know?

Salmon is an excellent source of vitamin B12, vitamin D, and selenium. It is a good source of niacin, omega-3 fatty acids, protein, phosphorus, and vitamin B6. It is also a good source of choline, pantothenic acid, biotin, and potassium.

Salmon Cookie Recipes

Recipe Ingredients for Salmon Cookies (6 servings)

  • 10 oz BUMBLE BEE Skinless and Boneless Pink Salmon
  • 2 tbsp chopped Onions
  • 1 tsp Dill Weed, Dried
  • 0.5 cup Cornmeal, Degermed, Enriched, Yellow

Recipe Directions for Salmon Cookies

  • Drain water from salmon.
  • Mince/dice onion.
  • Mix in mixing bowl. Add dill.
  • Add 1/4 cup cornmeal (and flour if you wish just make it a 50/50 ratio).Add cornmeal a bit at a time until the mixture can be formed into patties and still stay together.
  • Line cookie sheet. Bake at 350-375 for 40 minutes.
